About Us

At The AfterMint Co., we bring you the authentic taste of India’s favorite mouth freshener – Mukhwas. Handcrafted with love, our blends combine traditional recipes with the finest quality ingredients, ensuring every spoonful is fresh, flavorful, and full of goodness. From refreshing fennel seeds to aromatic herbs and natural sweeteners, we carefully curate each mix to promote digestion and leave a lasting taste of freshness. Whether it’s after a hearty meal, a family gathering, or just a moment to treat yourself, The AfterMint Co. is here to make every bite memorable. Why Choose Us? 🌱 100% natural ingredients 🛡️ Hygienically prepared & packed 🍬 Authentic taste, modern packaging 📦 Fresh delivery at your doorstep Because freshness isn’t just a taste – it’s an experience. 💚.

Since 2018, The AfterMint Co. has been crafting premium-quality Mukhwas using age-old recipes and the freshest ingredients. What started as a small passion for traditional mouth fresheners has now grown into a trusted brand loved by customers across India. Our mission is simple — to keep the tradition of Mukhwas alive while ensuring it meets today’s quality and hygiene standards. Each blend is made in small batches, ensuring maximum freshness, aroma, and taste in every pack.
